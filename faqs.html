Here are your frequently asked questions(FAQs)

How can I list my event on Eventopia?

To list your event, please contact us via the Contact page or send an email to events@eventopiakenya.com.

Are the events free to attend?

Some events are free, while others require ticket purchases. Each event page will have details on pricing and registration.

What types of events are featured?

We focus on sports events (marathons, races), fitness expos, and martial arts competitions happening across Kenya.

Can I get a refund for my ticket?

Refund policies depend on the ticket provider. Please check with the official ticket vendor for refund details.
